Revolutionizing EV Travel

ElectriStay makes EV travel simple by offering **overnight charging** at partnered hotels. With a **flat-rate pricing model**, guests can conveniently charge their vehicles while they sleep— ensuring a **fully charged car** and a **hassle-free journey** the next day.

Our Mission: Seamless EV Charging for Travelers

Our goal is to make **EV charging as easy as booking a hotel stay**. We connect travelers with **EV-friendly hotels**, ensuring they have access to **reliable overnight charging** at a predictable cost. No surprise fees, no complicated billing—just a **straightforward, worry-free experience**.

Our Core Values

Convenience

EV travelers can **pre-book charging stations** alongside their hotel stay, eliminating range anxiety.

Plan your journey better with our new feature that highlights **charging stations with nearby dining options**.

Transparent Pricing

Our **flat-rate overnight charging** ensures no hidden fees—what you see is what you pay.

Sustainability
