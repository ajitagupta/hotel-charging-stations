About ElectriStay

Building a sustainable travel future by connecting electric vehicle drivers with eco-friendly accommodations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to drive sustainable travel by offering easy access to hotels that provide EV charging stations. As we look toward the future, we strive to make electric vehicle travel more accessible and hassle-free for everyone.

Our Impact

We believe in a future where sustainable travel is the norm. Through our platform, we help reduce carbon emissions by promoting eco-friendly accommodation options and providing electric vehicle drivers with the tools they need to travel with ease.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We’re committed to reducing carbon footprints through sustainable travel solutions.

Innovation

We innovate to make electric vehicle travel easier and more convenient for everyone.

Collaboration

We believe in working together to create a brighter, cleaner future for travel.

Our Team

We’re a team of travel enthusiasts, sustainability advocates, and tech experts dedicated to revolutionizing the way people travel with electric vehicles. Together, we’re building a platform that connects travelers to the best eco-friendly accommodations.
